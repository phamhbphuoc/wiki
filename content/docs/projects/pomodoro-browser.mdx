---
title: Pomodoro Browswer Extension
---

A browser extension that helps you stay healthy and productive by reminding you to stand up and take breaks every 25 minutes using the Pomodoro Technique.

## Features

- ⏱️ **25-minute work sessions** followed by 5-minute breaks
- 🔔 **Browser notifications** to remind you to stand up and take breaks
- 🎯 **Simple interface** with start, pause, and reset controls
- 📊 **Visual timer** showing remaining time in the extension badge
- 🌐 **Cross-browser compatible** — works on Firefox, Chrome, Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers
